## 3. Market Position
**Zuständiger Spezialist:** Market Analysis Specialist

Apple maintains a dominant position in the premium consumer electronics market through its integrated ecosystem.

### **Apple's Strengths & Weaknesses**

#### **Strengths**
1.  **Powerful Ecosystem ("Walled Garden"):** Seamless integration between hardware and software creates high switching costs.
2.  **Brand Power & Loyalty:** Unmatched brand value and customer retention.
3.  **Unmatched Profitability:** Focus on premium segments captures the majority of industry profits.
4.  **Control over Silicon:** Apple Silicon (M/A-series) provides a performance and efficiency edge.

#### **Weaknesses**
1.  **Premium Pricing:** Limits market share in price-sensitive developing economies.
2.  **iPhone Dependence:** Still accounts for approximately 50% of total revenue.
3.  **Regulatory Pressure:** Increasing scrutiny over App Store policies and antitrust (EU DMA).
4.  **Supply Chain Risk:** Heavy reliance on China for manufacturing.
